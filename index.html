Practice 01

Technology Consulting

Decisions, not deliverables. We help leadership teams cut through vendor noise and pick the architecture that fits the business they actually run.

Every engagement starts with structured discovery: what you have, what it costs you to maintain, what it's blocking, and what it should be doing twelve months from now. We don't sell software and we don't resell platforms, so our advice doesn't come with a commission attached. The output is a written strategy, a phased roadmap, and — if you want us to stay — a delivery team to execute it.

IT Strategy & Roadmap

A 12–36 month technology plan tied to commercial objectives, with explicit build-versus-buy calls and budget guardrails at every phase.

Digital Transformation

Process redesign for teams moving from manual or legacy operations to digital-first workflows, including change-management support so the new stack actually gets adopted.

Systems Integration

Connecting CRMs, ERPs, payment gateways, marketing tools, and internal services so data flows without manual exports, CSVs, or weekly reconciliation meetings.

Cloud & Infrastructure Advisory

Cloud architecture reviews (AWS, Azure, GCP), cost optimisation, and migration planning with lock-in treated as a cost, not a feature.

Vendor Selection & RFP

Shortlisting platforms, drafting RFPs, evaluating bids, and sitting in on contract negotiations as your technical counterpart, not the vendor's.

Security & Compliance Baseline

Practical hardening of your stack against common threats, plus a checklist for the regulations you actually face (PCI-DSS, GDPR, UAE PDPL).

What you get

  • ✓ A written strategy document you can share with your board, your bank, or your investors.
  • ✓ A roadmap sequenced by ROI, not by what was easiest to scope first.
  • ✓ Honest recommendations — including the cases where the answer is "don't build anything yet".
  • ✓ An engineering-ready specification the delivery team can pick up and run.

Practice 02

E-commerce Solutions

We build online stores that hold up under real traffic, real catalogs, and real ops teams. No templates dressed up as custom work.

We treat e-commerce as a system, not a website. That means a storefront your customers can find and convert on, an admin your team can actually use on a Monday morning, and back-office plumbing that talks to your warehouse, your accountant, and your marketing stack. We pick the platform based on your catalog size, your growth curve, and your in-house technical depth — then we ship the integrations that make the difference.

Platform Setup & Migration

Launch on Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento (Adobe Commerce), or BigCommerce — or migrate cleanly from a legacy platform with zero order or customer data loss.

Headless & Composable Commerce

Decoupled front-ends (Next.js, Nuxt, Hydrogen) on top of commerce backends, for brands that need full design control and multi-channel reuse.

Marketplace Integrations

Amazon, Noon, Carrefour UAE, and other regional marketplaces connected to your central catalog, inventory, and pricing — no more five spreadsheets.

Payments, Tax & Logistics

Payment gateway setup, fraud rules, tax configuration for GCC and cross-border, and courier integrations (Aramex, DHL, Fetchr, regional last-mile).

Conversion Rate Optimisation

Audit-driven improvements to product pages, checkout, search, and merchandising, measured against a baseline captured before any change ships.

Store Operations & Support

Retainer-based maintenance, platform upgrades, performance monitoring, and a named technical contact who actually picks up the phone.

What you get

  • ✓ A storefront your team can launch, run, and update without calling us for every minor change.
  • ✓ Integrations that hold up against real order volume, not demo volume.
  • ✓ Documentation, training, and a handover pack your operations team can use from day one.
  • ✓ A measurement framework so you know whether last quarter's changes are moving revenue.

Practice 03

Data Classification & Analytics

Raw data is a liability. Structured data is an asset. We build the layer in between.

Most companies sit on more data than they can use — scattered across CRMs, spreadsheets, product databases, marketing tools, and internal documents. We start by classifying what is actually there: what is it, where does it live, how reliable is it, who is allowed to see it, and what is it for. From there we design the pipelines that keep it clean, the tagging systems that make it searchable, and the dashboards that make decisions faster.

Data Classification Pipelines

Automated and rules-based classification of documents, products, transactions, and content into structured taxonomies that downstream tools can rely on.

Content & Product Tagging

Consistent tagging across catalogs, knowledge bases, and media libraries — including multilingual and Arabic-language content with proper locale handling.

ML-Based Categorisation

Supervised and semi-supervised models for auto-categorisation, entity extraction, and sentiment scoring, with human-in-the-loop review.

Analytics Dashboards

Executive, operational, and product dashboards built on the BI stack that fits your environment — Looker, Power BI, Metabase, or a custom build.

Data Quality & Governance

Data profiling, deduplication, lineage tracking, and policy enforcement so your reports can be trusted by finance, the board, and your auditors.

Privacy & Compliance

Mapping data flows against GDPR, UAE PDPL, and sector-specific rules, including data subject request handling, retention policies, and breach response.

What you get

  • ✓ A single, queryable view of the data you already have, with documented quality and known gaps.
  • ✓ Classification that scales — humans define the rules once, automation enforces them every day.
  • ✓ Dashboards your team opens every morning because they answer the questions they're actually asking.
  • ✓ An audit trail for compliance reviews, not a binder no one opens.

About

A Dubai-based delivery team for operators who need things to actually work.

WHALE TECH — FZCO is a Dubai-based technology company operating from the IFZA freezone in Dubai Silicon Oasis. Founded in 2023, we were built to give mid-sized operators access to the same calibre of technology, e-commerce, and data capability that large enterprises buy from global firms — without the overhead, the retainer model, or the three-month discovery phase.

We do any IT development: from custom web platforms and mobile apps to backend services, integrations, automations, dashboards, and data pipelines. Behind the work sit dedicated cross-functional teams — strategy, engineering, e-commerce, data, QA and operations — plus the vendor partnerships, platform expertise, and infrastructure access needed to ship and run production systems. Whether the brief is a single integration or a multi-track build, we have the resources to staff it.

We work with founders, COOs, and CTOs who need a partner that can move between strategy and execution inside the same engagement. Our clients include regional retailers expanding online, service businesses formalising their data, and growth-stage companies rebuilding a stack that has outgrown its first version. We deliver across the UAE and internationally, with most work handled remotely and on-site presence arranged when a project requires it.
