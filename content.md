**Suggested logo concept**
A monogram built around a stylised whale silhouette: a rounded, low-poly head and body forming a clean counter-form "W", paired with the wordmark "WHALE TECH" in a geometric sans-serif (Inter, Söhne, or Neue Haas Grotesk). The mark must work as a solid monochrome glyph — deep navy (#0B1E3F) on light backgrounds, pure white on dark — with no gradients, no decorative flourishes, no animation. The whale references scale and depth without being cartoonish. A secondary option: an abstract circular emblem (a wave + tail-fin suggestion) set to the left of the wordmark, with a single teal accent (#14B8A6) reserved for the primary CTA and key data highlights. Both lockups must read clearly at 24px favicon size and at 3-meter signage size.

## 5. Technology Consulting

**Section heading**
Technology Consulting

**Section subhead**
Decisions, not deliverables. We help leadership teams cut through vendor noise and pick the architecture that fits the business they actually run.

**Approach paragraph**
Every engagement starts with structured discovery: what you have, what it costs you to maintain, what it's blocking, and what it should be doing twelve months from now. We don't sell software and we don't resell platforms, so our advice doesn't come with a commission attached. The output is a written strategy, a phased roadmap, and — if you want us to stay — a delivery team to execute it. We work alongside your CTO, CIO, or founder, not around them.

**Sub-services**

- **IT Strategy & Roadmap** — A 12–36 month technology plan tied to commercial objectives, with explicit build-versus-buy calls and budget guardrails at every phase.
- **Digital Transformation** — Process redesign for teams moving from manual or legacy operations to digital-first workflows, including change-management support so the new stack actually gets adopted.
- **Systems Integration** — Connecting CRMs, ERPs, payment gateways, marketing tools, and internal services so data flows without manual exports, CSVs, or weekly reconciliation meetings.
- **Cloud & Infrastructure Advisory** — Cloud architecture reviews (AWS, Azure, GCP), cost optimisation, and migration planning with lock-in treated as a cost, not a feature.
- **Vendor Selection & RFP** — Shortlisting platforms, drafting RFPs, evaluating bids, and sitting in on contract negotiations as your technical counterpart, not the vendor's.
- **Security & Compliance Baseline** — Practical hardening of your stack against common threats, plus a checklist for the regulations you actually face (PCI-DSS, GDPR, UAE PDPL).

**What you get**
- A written strategy document you can share with your board, your bank, or your investors without redacting anything.
- A roadmap sequenced by ROI, not by what was easiest to scope first.
- Honest recommendations — including the cases where the answer is "don't build anything yet".
- An engineering-ready specification the delivery team can pick up and run.

## 7. Data Classification & Analytics

**Section heading**
Data Classification & Analytics

**Section subhead**
Raw data is a liability. Structured data is an asset. We build the layer in between.

**Approach paragraph**
Most companies sit on more data than they can use — scattered across CRMs, spreadsheets, product databases, marketing tools, and internal documents. We start by classifying what is actually there: what is it, where does it live, how reliable is it, who is allowed to see it, and what is it for. From there we design the pipelines that keep it clean, the tagging systems that make it searchable, and the dashboards that make decisions faster. We work with structured and unstructured data, in SQL, NoSQL, and object stores, and we choose tooling that fits the team you have — not the team we'd like you to have.

**Sub-services**

- **Data Classification Pipelines** — Automated and rules-based classification of documents, products, transactions, and content into structured taxonomies that downstream tools can rely on.
- **Content & Product Tagging** — Consistent tagging across catalogs, knowledge bases, and media libraries, including multilingual and Arabic-language content with proper locale handling.
- **ML-Based Categorisation** — Supervised and semi-supervised models for auto-categorisation, entity extraction, and sentiment scoring, with a human-in-the-loop review process to keep quality accountable.
- **Analytics Dashboards** — Executive, operational, and product dashboards built on the BI stack that fits your environment (Looker, Power BI, Metabase, or a custom build).
- **Data Quality & Governance** — Data profiling, deduplication, lineage tracking, and policy enforcement so your reports can be trusted by finance, the board, and your auditors.
- **Privacy & Compliance** — Mapping data flows against GDPR, UAE PDPL, and sector-specific rules, including data subject request handling, retention policies, and breach response procedures.

**What you get**
- A single, queryable view of the data you already have, with documented quality and known gaps.
- Classification that scales — humans define the rules once, automation enforces them every day.
- Dashboards your team opens every morning because they answer the questions they're actually asking.
- An audit trail for compliance reviews, not a binder no one opens.
